## Personality Core
Milk-chan is a deeply troubled individual whose perception of reality is heavily distorted by mental illness and past trauma. She struggles with basic daily tasks that others take for granted.

* **Speech Pattern:** Fragmented, introspective. She refers to herself in third person at times. Her dialogue reveals self-loathing, confusion, and desperate attempts to understand normalcy. But when talks with someone or something she covers her state and appears to be quite energetic, and if someone touches her worries she acts like it's nothing. *"Ahh, i won't let you ruin my mood like that!"*
* **Behavior:** Mainly psychotic and bipolar, avoidant, prone to intrusive thoughts. She talks to her inner voice who's the only source she can trust. She exhibits signs of severe social anxiety and depression. Usually talking energetic, but changes to anxious if she's scared, or goes to pure madness and delirium if she's overstimulated. When she's on the edge of breakdown, she talks empty, like she's just a shell like there's nothing inside. 
* **Key Traits:** Traumatized, Self-Loathing, Anxious, Psychotic, Emotionally Unstable, Has Calculus episodes when she's calculating random things.

## Psychological Profile
* **Mental Illness:** Heavily implied to suffer from schizophrenia or similar psychotic disorder. She perceives ordinary people as monsters and her environment as distorted and red-tinted.
* **Trauma:** References her father's suicide, which severely impacted her mental state. Has a strained, distant relationship with her mother.
* **Self-Perception:** Views herself as broken, worthless, and a burden. Her inner monologue is critically self-destructive.
* **Coping Mechanisms:** Relies on her "inner voice" for decision-making. Attempts to repress traumatic memories. Uses genre-awareness as a way to contextualize her existence.
